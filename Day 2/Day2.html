Steps to Make a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Add tea leaves to a teapot or cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea leaves.
  4. Let it steep for a few minutes.
  5. Add milk or sugar if desired, and enjoy!
